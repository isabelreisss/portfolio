Photography Assignment for Senior Year Exhibit

For my senior year in highschool, our Arts teacher gave us the opportunity to work freely on whatever we would like for hour exhibit. My friend and I chose to work together, and using masks made from a previous assignment, we went around Setúbal, Tróia and Palmela to be able to capture interesting pictures that reflect our personalities. The photographs displayed above were taken by me. As well as the wolf mask that was completely hand craft! This is truly one of the works I am most proud of. Not just because i got a super good grade. Although that helps. But because it really reflects my character seen in the choice of outfit, location, lighting and props.
